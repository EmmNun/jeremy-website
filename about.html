My name is Jeremy Ortiz

I'm 25 years old and from New York. For the past 7 years I've worked in wrapping, printing, and art design, using a range of tools and techniques to deliver the best possible result for every client.

Every project starts with the same question: what does this surface, space, or brand actually need to say? From there it's concept, material, and craft — print runs, vehicle wraps, signage, and custom art direction, all built to hold up close-up and at a distance.
